Junchen Liu 刘俊辰

I'm a 4th-year undergraduate in the school of Computer Science, Beihang University. Currently, I'm also a visiting student at UC Berkeley, working closely with Ruilong Li under the guidance of Prof. Angjoo Kanazawa. I was a research intern at Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University supervised by Hao Zhao. I'm interested in computer vision and graphics.

I believe the real world can be modeled as a probability distribution of clusters on a high-dimensional manifold, suggesting the existence of a universal, succinct 3D representation that is both compact and non-scene-specific. I'm working on it.
